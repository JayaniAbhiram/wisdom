Principal's Desk

Dear Children, as you study and move forward with a resilient spirit to fulfil your dreams and make your parents proud and happy, do not forget to be a good human being. We know that the past year and months have brought in a lot of misery, unhappiness and misfortune to many. Let us be aware of the poor, the under privileged, the less fortunate, and try to help them whenever possible. These days you are at home, so do not become a burden to your parents and elders. Help them, listen to them, do not waste time in doing or watching useless things, rather invest your time in doing something innovative, creative and worthwhile that will be beneficial to you, for your future endeavor.

Mrs.Sushila Bhati

Principal
